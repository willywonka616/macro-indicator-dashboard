## The headline finding: general, not central, government is the closer basis match

`TASKeuroarea.md` §3 expected central government to be the basis Dalio's
85% figure uses. Fetching both live at his own March-2025 vintage found
the opposite: **general government (87.7%) is 2.7 points from 85%;
central government (77.6%) is 7.4 points off**. `build_eur()` doesn't
hardcode either assumption — it computes both and adopts whichever is
closer, dynamically; this run that logic correctly identified general
government. Reported exactly as found, not adjusted to fit the original
expectation.
